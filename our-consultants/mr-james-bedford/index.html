Mr James D Bedford MSc FRCS(Plast) BrDipHandSurg

Biography

James Bedford is Specialty Lead Consultant for Burns & Plastic Surgery at Manchester University NHS Foundation Trust. He joined the department in 2018 following training in plastic and hand surgery in the North West, followed by a National Advanced Training Fellowship in Hand Surgery in Leeds. His special interests are in children's hand surgery and nerve surgery including brachial plexus injury and spasticity. He undertakes specialist clinics for the Greater Manchester Brachial Plexus service, the Children's Hand, Elbow and Shoulder Surgery (CHESS) service, and at the Specialised Ability Centre. Mr Bedford enjoyes collaborating with surgeons in diverse specialties including orthopaedics, neurosurgery, general surgery and cardiothoracics for patients who require complex soft tissue reconstruction.

Special Interests

  • Congenital and children's hand surgery and microsurgery
  • Peripheral nerve injury including brachial plexus
  • Spasticity of the hand and tetraplegia
  • Limb and general complex reconstruction
