Our clinics

Each of our consultants with their trainees runs various dedicated specialist clinics to provide specialized care to our patients. Here are some of our key clinics:

Brachial Plexus/Nerve Clinic

This clinic is dedicated to patients with injuries of the main nerves to the upper limb and nerve problems such as tumors.

Burns Outreach Clinic

Our award-winning Burns Outreach Team provides assessment, management, and follow-up for patients with burn injuries.


Children’s Hand Clinic

Our pediatric hand clinic is dedicated to treating children with congenital hand anomalies and providing follow-up care for those who have undergone surgery for traumatic hand injuries.

Cleft Lip and Palate Clinic

Our cleft lip and palate clinic, led by experienced surgeons, offers specialized care for patients with these conditions. We provide comprehensive treatment and work closely with a multidisciplinary team.


Complex Wound Clinic/Outreach

Our complex wound clinic, staffed by specialist nurses, manages challenging wounds using the latest wound care products and dressings. We also provide an outreach service, visiting patients in peripheral hospitals or at home.

Hand Therapy Clinic

Our dedicated hand therapy clinic is led by senior therapists with specialized qualifications in hand management. We offer exercises, splinting, and other therapies to optimize functional outcomes for patients before and after hand surgery.


Neurofibromatosis Clinic

Our clinic provides care for patients with neurofibromatosis, a genetic disorder leading to nerve sheath tumors. Led by experienced surgeons, we offer comprehensive treatment and collaborate with other specialists.

Oncoplastic Breast Clinic

Our oncoplastic breast clinic, conducted jointly with oncological breast surgeons, focuses on the treatment of breast cancer and breast reconstruction. We provide a comprehensive approach to care in collaboration with the Nightingale Centre.


Telemedicine Clinic

Our telemedicine clinic allows remote consultations, facilitating follow-up care and assessments without the need for patients to travel long distances. This efficient approach enhances accessibility and convenience for patients.

Wrist Clinic

Our wrist clinic, staffed by consultants with expertise in hand and wrist problems, specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of wrist pain and complex wrist injuries. We provide comprehensive care tailored to individual needs.
