Services at other hospitals

In order to maximize ease of access to the services provided by the Manchester Centre for Plastic Surgery and Burns, many of our consultants hold clinic and have theatre lists in hospitals outside our hub sites at UHSM and RMCH. Patients seen at their local hospital who have more complex problems requiring the use of our specialist surgical equipment will be admitted to UHSM for surgery. However, aftercare clinic follow-up will usually be offered at the site most convenient for the patient.

We have clinics at the following sites:

  • Manchester Royal Infirmary Doesn't offer surgery
  • Rochdale Infirmary Offers surgery
  • Royal Bolton Hospital Offers surgery
  • Royal Oldham Hospital Offers surgery
  • Salford Royal Hospital Offers surgery
  • Tameside General Hospital Doesn't offer surgery
