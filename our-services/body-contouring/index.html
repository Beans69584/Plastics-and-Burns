Body contouring

As more people access the NHS for bariatric (weight loss) surgery such as gastric banding or bypass, we are seeing an increasing number of patients with skin and soft tissue excess after massive weight loss. We offer a range of surgical interventions for these individuals. However, specific CCG funding needs to be approved before referring such patients to the Manchester Centre for Plastic Surgery and Burns. Additionally, a patient's weight should be stable for two years before referral, and their nutrition should be optimized with regard to electrolytes and micronutrients. We are able to offer the following procedures:

Body Contouring Procedures

  • Abdominoplasty/Flur-de-Lis abdominoplasty
  • Thigh lift
  • Brachioplasty
  • Belt lipectomy
  • Apronectomy (in the context of assisting another surgical specialty with access to the abdomen or pelvis)
