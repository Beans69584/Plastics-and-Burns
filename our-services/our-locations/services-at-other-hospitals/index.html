Services at other hospitals

In order to maximise ease of access to the services provided by the Manchester Centre for Plastic Surgery and Burns many of our consultants hold clinic and have theatre lists in hospitals out with our hub sites at UHSM and RMCH.   Patients seen at their local hospital who have more complex problems requiring use of our specialist surgical equipment will be admitted to UHSM for surgery, however aftercare clinic follow up will usually be offered at the site most convenient for the patient.

We have clinics at the following sites (Those marked with an asterix also offer surgery)

  • Manchester Royal Infirmary
  • Rochdale Infirmary*
  • Royal Bolton Hospital*
  • Royal Oldham Hospital*
  • Salford Royal Hospital*
  • Tameside General Hospital
