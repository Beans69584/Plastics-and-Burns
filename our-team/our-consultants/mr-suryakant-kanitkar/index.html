Mr Suryakant Kanitkar

Consultant Plastic Surgeon

  • Biography

Biography

Suryakant is on the GMC specialist register in Plastic Surgery. He trained in the UK and India in the whole spectrum of the specialty, and has a held consultant posts in India and the UK over more than 20 years. Suryakant has worked and visited various units in the two countries as well as in USA to acquire a variety of international exposure and experience. He is and has been a committed teacher and mentor for trainees all through his career, and continues to be involved in voluntary work in India for underprivileged people.

Special Interests

  • Children’s Plastic and Reconstructive surgery: congenital defects, prominent ears, children’s burns, multidisciplinary team reconstructions
  • Skin Cancer treatment including reconstruction
  • General plastic surgery – reconstructive and cosmetic
  • Body contouring including breast surgery and major reconstruction of trunk defects
  • Burn and post burn deformity reconstruction
  • Limb trauma reconstruction

Clinic times & locations

  • UHSM (Wythenshawe), alternate Friday mornings
  • RMCH (Children's Hospital), Wednesday mornings
  • CMFT (Manchester Royal Infirmary, adults), alternate Thursday afternoons
